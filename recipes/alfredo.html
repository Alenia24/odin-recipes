Shrimp Alfredo

Description

Shrimp Alfredo is a perfect meal for those who love seafood and pasta. The creamy and velvety texture of the Alfredo sauce is what makes it a luxurious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Shrimp
  • 3 tablesppon butter
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • Fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cups Heavy whipping cream
  • 2-3 oz cream cheese
  • 1-11/2 freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 8 oz Fettucine
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet with 2 teaspoons of oil, then add shrimp and let it rest for 30 seconds then flip.
  2. Add Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook for 4-5 minutes, making sure to flip to cook each side until fully cooked. Set aside.
  4. Heat skillet on medium heat , then add butter to the pan, followed by garlic, thyme and Italian seasoning. Saute for about 1-2 minutes, or until garlic is soft and fragrant.
  5. Lower heat to medium-low. Whisk in cream and milk, then add cream cheese while continuing to whisk until smooth. Reduce heat to simmer, then season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Continue to simmer for about 3-4 minutes until the sauce starts to thicken. Turn off the heat and toss with pasta and shrimp to serve.
